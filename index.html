Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Download NeonGlide from the App Store, launch the app, and follow the on-screen tutorial to jump right in.

Is NeonGlide free to play?

NeonGlide is free to download. Some optional in-app purchases may be available to enhance your experience.

My progress was lost. What can I do?

Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Game Center account you used previously. If the issue persists, please contact us and we'll do our best to help.

How do I report a bug or a problem?

Please reach out via the contact form below or send us an email with a description of the issue and your device model. Screenshots or screen recordings are always welcome!

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to submit a refund request.
