Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NeonGlide ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the NeonGlide mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

NeonGlide is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

  • We do not require account registration.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact details.
  • We do not track your precise location.

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device and, if you opt in, through Apple's iCloud or Game Center services, which are governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

2. Automatically Collected Data

The App may collect anonymized, aggregated analytics data (such as crash reports and usage statistics) solely for the purpose of improving app performance and stability. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

3. Third-Party Services

If the App uses any third-party SDKs or services (e.g., Apple Game Center, StoreKit), those services are subject to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

4. Children's Privacy

NeonGlide does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately.

5. Data Security

Because we do not collect personal data, there is minimal risk of a data breach affecting your personal information. All local data stored on your device is protected by your device's built-in security.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
